About

I love technology, I always have. My first computer was a TRS-80 with Level 1 BASIC and 4K of RAM. It had a cassette recorder as a storage device with RCA type cables. I remember coding the computer to read, and write to the tape, it was so slow, yet so high tech! My father is architect (the building kind) and mother a fine artist and a decorator of sorts, she did a great job painting "Jonathon Livingston Seagull" or so I thought when I was 10.

I am creative technologist, and try to use technology to simplify my life. I embrace new technology and search for ways to re-purpose old technology.

Steve Jobs and Walt Disney, two of the greatest men of all times. I often wonder what they would build or dream up together. They focused on the experience, the user's experience. The experience was everything.

As a manager, recruiter and builder of teams and training programs I try to focus on what the user is seeing and thinking and make it enjoyable. As a father, I try to make the time I am able to spend with my family enjoyable and give them a good experience.

As Regional Director with Phoenix Staff, my goals are to develop an internal team that will not only find you the best talent, but also provide you with an experience so great and successful that you will want to share with your colleagues.

At Work

I am the Regional Director for Phoenix Staff, a boutique staffing firm focusing on high caliber IT talent. We have offices in Phoenix, Las Vegas and Austin. Our business is split between fulltime direct hire, contract to hire, and pure contract opportunities. We have the opportunity to help those that need sponsorship on a limited basis.

I manage the Las Vegas and Austin locations, operating from the bottom line. I am responsible for managing our current business, scouting new business and creating new products and services, as well as identifying, hiring and mentoring our team. I have found and trained over 80% of our current teams in Sales, Recruting and Sourcing.

I have built recruiting teams, compensation plans, training and retention programs. My training programs are tailored to specific roles within the organization and are built for iPad.

I manage recruiting and sourcing teams in mutliple states as well as India, and work with development resources in Mexico.

Additionally I research and deploy new company wide technology solutions to keep us effecient and using technology to improve our customer experience and business's bottom line.

Social Media

No trend in the past ten years has been more perplexing — or necessary — to businesses than social media. In fact,a recent report from socialmediatoday.com indicates that 90% of small business are active on social sites, and nearly all big businesses have a presence. Why? These sites are a low-cost channel for marketing and communication with your customers. But, unlike traditional marketing channels, social sites are ever evolving and must be maintained in real-time. Therefore, maintaining a respectable social presence requires time and money.
